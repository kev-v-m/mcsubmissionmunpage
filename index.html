WHAT

Model United Nations is an extra-curricular activity in which students typically roleplay delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees. This activity takes place at MUN conferences, which is usually organized by a high school or college MUN club.

WHY

MUN provides delegates a platform in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, International policies and politics, and the United Nations.
